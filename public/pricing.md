# OfferAdvisor Pricing

Last updated: May 2026

OfferAdvisor uses one-time pricing — no subscriptions, no recurring charges.

## Free
- Price: $0 (create an account)
- Includes: 1 AI coaching session, 1 negotiation email script, BRIDGE tab (student benchmarks, offer compare, path explorer)

## Student Plus — BRIDGE for students & new grads
- Price: $19.99 USD, one-time, 30-day access
- Includes: Full BRIDGE workspace — salary benchmarks, side-by-side offer comparison, career path explorer, campus verification for partner universities
- Invite codes available for cohort pricing

## Offer Sprint — Professional toolkit
- Price: $29 USD, one-time, 30-day access
- Includes: Unlimited AI coaching sessions, negotiation scripts, salary benchmarks, counter-offer calculator, role-play practice, win tracker, multi-currency (USD, GBP, INR, EUR, CAD, AUD, SGD, AED)

## Offer in Hand — Lifetime Pro
- Price: $49 USD, one-time, no expiry
- Includes: Everything in Sprint + Templates tab, Playbook tab, History tab, voice mock interview with Alex, priority support, all future Pro features

All paid plans include a 30-day money-back guarantee. Payments via Stripe.
